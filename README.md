## ğŸ’» Usage

### For Teachers

1. **Setup**
   - Open the app in your browser
   - Select the appropriate period using the period tabs
   - Navigate to "Scenario Notes" to paste debate rules/topics

2. **During Debate**
   - Click student names to add them to the speaking queue
   - Timer auto-starts when you hit "Next" to move to the next speaker
   - Use "Discard" to remove current speaker without counting their turn
   - Use "Next" to complete their turn and move to the next person

3. **Tracking Participation**
   - Student names change color based on how many times they've spoken:
     - Gray: 0 times
     - Light brown: 1 time
     - Medium brown: 2 times
     - Darker brown: 3 times
     - Darkest: 4+ times
